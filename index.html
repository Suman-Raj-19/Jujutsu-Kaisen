Jujutsu Kaisen
Welcome to the world of curses and sorcerers.
A battle for survival begins now as high schooler Yuji Itadori joins a secret organization of sorcerers to eliminate a powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna.
Gojo Satoru
"Throughout Heaven and Earth, I alone am the honored one."
The strongest sorcerer alive, possessor of the Limitless and Six Eyes. He is the teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High and the ultimate deterrent against curses.
Yuji Itadori
A talented athlete who becomes the vessel for Sukuna.
Yuji swallowed a finger of Sukuna to save his friends. Despite the King of Curses living inside him, he maintains his humanity and kindness.
Megumi Fushiguro
A first-year student who uses the Ten Shadows Technique.
A descendant of the Zenin clan, Megumi can summon various Shikigami like Divine Dogs and Max Elephant to fight alongside him.
Nobara Kugisaki
A brash sorcerer using a hammer and nails.
She moved to Tokyo from a rural village. Her Straw Doll Technique allows her to strike curses from a distance by using resonance.
Toge Inumaki
A cursed speech user who speaks in rice ball ingredients.
His speech is infused with cursed energy, forcing anything he says to happen. To avoid hurting others, he uses a limited vocabulary like 'Salmon' and 'Tuna'.
Toji Fushiguro
The "Sorcerer Killer" with zero cursed energy.
Born with a Heavenly Restriction, he traded all his cursed energy for god-like physical strength and speed. He is Megumi's father.
Kento Nanami
A former salaryman turned Grade 1 sorcerer.
He uses the 7:3 Ratio Technique to create a weak point on his targets. He views being a sorcerer as "shit," but work is "shit" too.
Suguru Geto
A special grade curse user and former friend of Gojo.
He specializes in Cursed Spirit Manipulation. His goal is to eliminate all non-sorcerers to create a world without cursed spirits.
Mahito
A cursed spirit born from human hatred.
His Idle Transfiguration allows him to reshape souls, including his own. He is playful yet incredibly cruel and dangerous.
